Price Formula

Sell Price = Base × Distance × City Modifier × Language Modifier × Religion Modifier

All modifiers stack multiplicatively. Distance adds +5% per city hop away from the source. Your native language always gives the Fluent (+5%) bonus at cities that speak it.
